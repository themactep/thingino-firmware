Product Overview
----------------

The AiW4211L is a highly integrated 2.4GHz low-power SoC WiFi chip that integrates IEEE 802.11b/g/n baseband and RF circuitry. The RF circuitry includes modules such as power amplifier PA, low-noise amplifier LNA, RF balun, antenna switch, and power management. It supports a standard bandwidth of 20MHz and a narrow bandwidth of 5MHz/10MHz, providing a maximum physical layer rate of 72.2Mbit/s.

The AiW4211L WiFi baseband supports Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiplexing (OFDM) technology and is backward compatible with Direct Sequence Spread Spectrum (DSSS) and Complement Keying (CCK) technologies, supporting various data rates of the IEEE 802.11 b/g/n protocol.

The AiW4211L chip integrates a high-performance 32-bit microprocessor, a hardware security engine, and a rich set of peripheral interfaces, including SPI, UART, I2C, PWM, GPIO, and multiple ADCs. It also supports a high-speed SDIO2.0 Slave interface with a maximum clock speed of 50MHz. The chip has built-in SRAM and Flash, which can run independently and support running programs on Flash.


Main Specifications
-------------------

1x1 2.4GHz full band, supports 5M/10M/20M bandwidth

Supports IEEE 802.11b/g/n protocol

Integrated TX/RX Switch, Balun

Supports STA and AP modes, and supports up to 6 STA accesses when used as AP

Supports NPA WPA/WPA2 personal and WPS2.0 wireless security protocols

CPU: High-performance 32-bit processor, maximum operating frequency 160MHz

Storage: 352KB RAM, 288KB ROM, 2MB Flash

Interface: SDIO/SPI/I2C/I2S/UART

Package: QFN-32, 5mm x 5mm

Operating temperature: -40 ℃~+85℃


Application areas
